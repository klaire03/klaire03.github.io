A Bit About Me

I’m Claire Economopoulou, a London-based data professional transitioning into Data Science. I work primarily with Python and SQL, using machine learning and data visualization to explore patterns in data and understand why things happen.

Before this, I spent seven years as a UX Researcher, using qualitative and quantitative methods across technology, finance, and education. That experience shaped how I approach data problems: start with the right questions, look for meaningful patterns, and focus on insights that help people make decisions.
