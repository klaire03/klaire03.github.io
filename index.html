About

I treat research as a service — one that changes what gets built, not just how it looks.

Seven-plus years across risk & compliance, consumer hardware, VR, security software and higher education — at Moody's Analytics, Meta Reality Labs, Google, Avast and the University of the Arts London.

I pair qualitative instinct with quantitative method: clustering, segmentation, A/B testing and inferential statistics sit alongside interviews, diary studies and service blueprints. Findings are framed by the business risk they carry, so the work lands on the roadmap rather than in a folder.

My background is in social policy and digital democracy (MSc, Stockholm University) — which is where the interest in how people decide, together, first started.
