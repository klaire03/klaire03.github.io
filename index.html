About

The analyst
behind the data

I’m Claire Economopoulou, a London-based data professional transitioning into Data Science. My work focuses on Python, SQL, machine learning, and data visualization, with a strong interest in uncovering patterns in large datasets and understanding the “why” behind user behavior.

My curiosity for data grew from seven years as a UX Researcher working on B2B and B2C products across technology, finance, and education. This experience shaped how I approach data problems: asking the right questions, choosing meaningful representations, and turning insights into value for real people and organizations.

I believe one of the most valuable skills in data science isn’t purely technical — it’s judgment: knowing when the model is good enough, when the insight is clear, and when it’s time to turn analysis into action.
